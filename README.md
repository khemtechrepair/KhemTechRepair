## 📂 Repair Knowledge - Ways to ensure you DON'T break your devices (Click to expand)
<details>
<summary><b>The "Frozen iPhone" Fix</b></summary>
Before you panic, try a force restart: Press Vol Up, then Vol Down, then hold the Side Power Button until the logo appears. Most software crashes and bugs are solved by this.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Saving your PS5/Xbox HDMI Port</b></summary>
The #1 cause of port failure is moving the console while the cable is still plugged in. Always unplug the HDMI *before* you rotate or move your console - and when doing so, try to be as gentle as possible and never 'yank' the cord.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>New iPad Air/Pro Bending</b></summary>
Yeah, we've seen JerryRigEverythiing's tests and our customers have bring in BENT iPads all the time. No iPad without a home button should be without a case - and try to get a case with structural integrity like the Apple iPad Magic Keyboard which (although expensive) is a great way to always be able to remove the iPad from its case easily, have a keyboard and trackpad on-the-go, and never have the iPad bend in a backpack. 
</details>
